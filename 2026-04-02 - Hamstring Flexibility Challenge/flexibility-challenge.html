🧘 Flexibility Challenge

Daily 8 minutes  ·  2 blocks of 4 min  ·  Pike + Pancake

🔷 BLOCK 1: THE PIKE 4:00
① Layered PNF Pike 1:00

Sit with legs straight. Fold to your first "wall".

TimePhaseAction
0:00–0:10CONTRACTDig heels into floor hard
0:10–0:30RELAXBreathe out, pull deeper. Stay.
0:30–0:40CONTRACTFrom new depth, dig heels again
0:40–1:00RELAXSink to final, deepest range
⚠️ Knees bending / back rounding = structural limit. Stay & breathe — don't sacrifice form for fake depth.
② Slow-Mo Jefferson Curls 1:30
💡 "Roll over a beach ball" — spine CURVES segmentally (NOT flat back). Chin → chest → roll down.
PhaseDurationCue
DOWN10sRoll down vertebra by vertebra
BOTTOM10sUse abs to pull forehead toward knees
UP10sTuck tailbone first, stack spine bottom-up
× 3 cycles
③ Pike Compression Lifts 1:30

Seated pike. Legs locked, toes pointed. Hands by knees (intermediate) or shins (advanced).

15 pulses → 15s hold (max height) → 15s rest → REPEAT ×2
🔶 BLOCK 2: THE PANCAKE 4:00
① Layered PNF Pancake 1:00

Sit in widest straddle. Lean forward.

TimePhaseAction
0:00–0:10CONTRACTHeels "pinch" toward each other (crush the floor)
0:10–0:30RELAXWalk hands further forward. Stay.
0:30–0:40CONTRACTFrom new depth, drive heels in again
0:40–1:00RELAXReach chest as close to floor as possible
② Pancake Good Mornings 1:30
💡 FLAT BACK (unlike the pike). Hinge at hips — belly button toward floor. Hands behind head or reaching forward.
PhaseDuration
DOWN5s
HOLD2s
UP5s
× 10 reps — hold rep 10 at bottom for 30 seconds
③ Straddle Compression Lifts 1:30

Seated straddle. Hands in front of groin. Lift both legs simultaneously. Do NOT lean back!

15 pulses → 15s hold (max height) → 15s rest → REPEAT ×2
⚡ Scaling Notes
SituationResponse
Quad cramp during compressionsNormal — nervous system adapting. Shake 2 seconds → keep going.
Want harder compressionsMove hands closer to feet (further = significantly harder).
Form breaking in pikeYou've hit today's structural limit — breathe and hold, don't push.
